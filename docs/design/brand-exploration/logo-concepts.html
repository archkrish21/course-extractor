About this sketch

This SVG is a direction/composition sketch, not a production-grade illustration. Hand-coded SVG can't realistically produce "rich, painterly" character art — it reads as flat/mascot-style at best. For a truly rich genie that looks commissioned, I'd recommend one of these paths:
  • AI image generator (Midjourney, DALL·E 3 via ChatGPT, Ideogram, Flux) — fastest; use the prompt below.
  • Hire an illustrator on Fiverr (~$50–150), 99designs, or Dribbble — most polished; 3–7 day turnaround.
  • Adobe Express / Canva Magic — middle ground; built-in mascot templates you can recolor.

Legal-safe AI prompts (three directions)

All three avoid the Disney Aladdin trade-dress cues: no blue skin, no magic lamp, no smoke-from-lamp tail. Each keeps the "wise, confident, magical helper" brand concept. Pick one and run it through Midjourney / DALL·E 3 / Ideogram.

Emerald-green skin, academic robes, standing on a book stack. Most clearly "academic."
