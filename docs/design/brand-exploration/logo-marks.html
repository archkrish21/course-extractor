My recommendation

Go with Option 1 — Turban + Gem.

It carries the mascot's visual DNA directly (plum turban, gold wrap, ruby gem), stays readable at 16px (unlike the mascot itself), and creates a clear brand system:
  • Mascot (full genie) → landing hero, marketing, email headers
  • Turban mark → favicon, nav bar, app icon, tiny contexts
  • Wordmark planwithgenie → text-heavy contexts (emails, invoices, footers)
Option 2 is safer but disconnected from the mascot. Option 3 (lamp) works but revives some of the "genie lamp" connotations we were trying to move away from.
